Rochell Hatfield

Hello,
I am a Creative Graphic Designer with User Experience capabilities and an expert in Brand Strategy. I also excel in business marketing and sales.

Graduated from Eastern Washington University with a Bachelor of Design majoring in Visual Communication and Design and a Certificate in User Experience/User Interface.

I have gained valuable skills in photography, poster and product design, coding, and painting among other skills.

At Eastern, I was a member of the EWU VCD club, presented my photography in the 2019 "Incandescent Exposure" student gallery, and was a lead designer and co-presenter for "Endolysins as a novel acne medication" at the 2020 EWU Research and Creative Works Symposium.
