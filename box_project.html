Beer
Packaging
Project

I designed a package for beer
that allows the taste to be
fresh from the tap.

My beer box packaging project was designed for my final grade in Production Design at Eastern Washington University. I am happy to say that I excelled in the class.

I conducted research, placed a lot of time and effort into the care of craft, and developed surface graphics and typesettings.

As a designer, I seek out ways to connect with it and solve the problem(s) critically with esthetic practicality. I achieve form + functionality.
