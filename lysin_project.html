Endolysin
Poster
Project

I designed the poster layout

This poster was designed for the start-up “Fage,” a student group at Eastern Washington University looking to make a new acne medication. I designed the poster to be presented at an open house for funds acquisition.
The first step was to understand the context of the presentation, so I researched the basic science behind their product so that I could better develop the imagery.
The next step was to create a creative brief that involved diving into more detail of the message, tone, people, audience, and then answering SWOT: strengths, weaknesses, opportunities, and threats that are involved.
I always make sure to start with a sketchbook and pencils to get initial ideas down. I thought of science being organic and natural, so I wanted to stay away from sharp edges and have the concept naturally flow. Especially since the presentation was towards an audience that has numerous levels of knowledge on the subject. The poster needed to connect easily with the viewer.
The final service included the large (# X #) poster, design guidelines, and a digital copy of the poster. The poster was praised at the open house, helping the client acquire $2,500 in funding.
